# Royal Braids / Salon Shop — Ad Video Script With Voice-Over

This script showcases the current **Supabase + Render + Vercel** Royal Braids salon platform.

## Video goal

Show Royal Braids as a complete digital salon platform for customers, staff, admins, bookings, content, communication, and security.

## Recording checklist

- Open the live Vercel site or local `public/index.html`.
- Open `public/admin.html` for the admin console.
- Use demo data only.
- Avoid showing private emails, phone numbers, API keys, JWTs, service-role keys, or customer data.
- Capture desktop and mobile views.

## Full showcase script

### Scene 1 — Opening hook

**Visuals:** Royal Braids splash screen and homepage hero.  
**Text overlay:** `Royal Braids — A Complete Digital Salon Platform`

**Voice-over:**

> Welcome to Royal Braids — a premium salon experience brought online through a complete modern platform for beauty, bookings, client care, and business management.

### Scene 2 — Homepage and services

**Visuals:** Navigation, hero, services, category tabs, “Book This Service”.  
**Text overlay:** `Elegant design • Services • Booking access`

**Voice-over:**

> Customers can explore salon services, browse categories, view prices and durations, and move directly from discovery to booking in a responsive, polished website.

### Scene 3 — Gallery and style discovery

**Visuals:** Gallery filters, featured rails, lightbox, before/after images.  
**Text overlay:** `Gallery • Filters • Favorites • Cloudinary media`

**Voice-over:**

> The gallery helps customers discover their next look with filters, featured styles, before-and-after images, and media managed through Cloudinary-backed workflows.

### Scene 4 — Online booking and waitlist

**Visuals:** Booking form, date/time/stylist fields, slot availability, waitlist prompt.  
**Text overlay:** `Slot-safe booking • Waitlist support • Notifications`

**Voice-over:**

> Booking workflows are powered by Render and Supabase. Customers choose a service and available slot, while backend transaction logic helps prevent double booking and can offer a waitlist when a slot is no longer available.

### Scene 5 — Client account and dashboard

**Visuals:** Auth modal, dashboard, appointments, favorites, profile, login history.  
**Text overlay:** `Supabase Auth • Client dashboard • Self-service`

**Voice-over:**

> Clients can sign in securely with Supabase Auth, manage appointments, save favorite styles, update account details, review security history, and use self-service actions like cancellation and rescheduling.

### Scene 6 — Reviews, blog, and contact

**Visuals:** Reviews, blog cards, contact form, location details.  
**Text overlay:** `Reviews • Blog • Contact messages`

**Voice-over:**

> The public website also supports reviews, moderated testimonials, salon blog content, and contact messages that flow into the backend and admin console.

### Scene 7 — Admin console access

**Visuals:** Admin login, permission-scoped tabs.  
**Text overlay:** `Private admin console • Role-based access`

**Voice-over:**

> Behind the customer experience is a private admin console protected by Supabase Auth and database-backed permissions, so staff only see and manage the sections they are allowed to access.

### Scene 8 — Booking operations

**Visuals:** Bookings tab, counters, filters, status actions.  
**Text overlay:** `Booking operations • Lifecycle-safe actions`

**Voice-over:**

> Staff can monitor appointment activity, filter by status, inspect customer details, and safely confirm, complete, cancel, release slots, or move waitlisted bookings through protected Render backend workflows.

### Scene 9 — Waitlist and schedule

**Visuals:** Waitlist queue, schedule day/week views, selected booking detail.  
**Text overlay:** `Waitlist queue • Day/week schedule`

**Voice-over:**

> Waitlist and schedule tools give staff a clear operational view of demand, available slots, and daily or weekly appointments.

### Scene 10 — Content management

**Visuals:** Gallery, blog, reviews, messages, services tabs.  
**Text overlay:** `Content • Reviews • Messages • Services`

**Voice-over:**

> The admin console includes content management for gallery styles, blog posts, reviews, contact messages, and service visibility, keeping the public website fresh without code changes.

### Scene 11 — Security and automation

**Visuals:** Security dashboard, logs, alerts, scheduler/job graphic if desired.  
**Text overlay:** `Security monitoring • Notification jobs • Audit logs`

**Voice-over:**

> Security monitoring, login activity, account changes, alerts, audit logs, and notification jobs are handled through the Supabase and Render architecture, with secrets kept server-side.

### Scene 12 — Tech stack close

**Visuals:** Architecture graphic or icons.  
**Text overlay:** `Supabase • Render • Vercel • Cloudinary • Resend • WhatsApp`

**Voice-over:**

> Royal Braids is built with a modern production stack: Supabase for data and auth, Render for trusted backend workflows, Vercel for frontend delivery, Cloudinary for media, and email and WhatsApp integrations for communication.

Staff get a private admin console for bookings, schedules, content, reviews, messages, services, users, and security. Built with Supabase, Render, Vercel, Cloudinary, email, and WhatsApp automation — Royal Braids turns a salon website into a full digital business system.
